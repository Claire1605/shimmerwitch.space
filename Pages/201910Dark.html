Dark

Oct 2019


This is a tiny prototype I made, which I think about quite a lot and will hopefully come back to. I created this art style in Unity using Fernando Ramallo's Doodle Studio 95.
Right now you walk down this long road, with high walls either side, always moving forwards. I've noticed that a lot of the small games I make in Unity and other tools are some form of flat game, 3D in this case. A path that you follow and take in the surroundings as you move, thinking about what you see.

For this game, I would like to experiment with music more than in my previous work. I'm inspired by games such as Pilgrimage by Joost Eggermont Jorrit de Vries, which allows you to explore the music of band La Dispute, by travelling through beautful abstract environments. Music is a large and personal part of my life, but something I have struggled with creatively over the last decade or so. When I come back to this project I would like to create a world designed around the music I write.
